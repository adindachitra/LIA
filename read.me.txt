# ⚖️ LIA – Legal Information Assistant

**LIA (Legal Information Assistant)** is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to improve access to legal information for **women, children, and Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)**. LIA helps users understand their legal rights, analyze legal documents, and receive easy-to-understand legal guidance through a conversational AI interface.

> **Know what you sign.**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Many people struggle to understand legal documents due to complex legal terminology and limited access to affordable legal assistance. LIA bridges this gap by providing AI-assisted legal information that is simple, accessible, and user-friendly.

LIA is intended as an educational and informational tool and **does not replace professional legal advice**.

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- 👩 Women
- 👧 Children
- 🏪 Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)
- 📚 General public seeking legal information

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 📄 Contract Analysis
  - Upload legal documents
  - Identify important clauses
  - Detect potential legal risks
  - Generate easy-to-understand summaries

- 💬 AI Legal Chat Assistant
  - Ask legal questions in natural language
  - Receive simplified legal explanations

- 📚 Legal Information Center
  - Women's rights
  - Children's rights
  - MSME regulations
  - Consumer protection
  - Employment law
  - Family law

- 🌐 Multilingual Support
  - Indonesian
  - English
  - *(Future development: additional regional languages)*
